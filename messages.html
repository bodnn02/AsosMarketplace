    • Уведомления 03.02 Uploading brand certificates: Advantages, requirements, and uploading Ozon sets special rules for uploading and selling some brands. In particular, we ask that you upload documents confirming your right to sell products of such brands. You can’t sell the brand’s products without certificates on Ozon, if they are brands with limited access products. Without certificates, such products will be hidden. However, even if your brand is not on the list of ones with limited access, uploading and verifying documents gives you additional benefits. 1 “Original product” mark. What does it mean? If you upload documents confirming your right to sell products to your account, and they pass the verification, then your products of the specified brand will be marked as "Original product" within a day. This checkmark will show customers that you are a verified seller and it’s safe to buy your products: they can be sure that they get a quality branded product, not a fake. 2 Hence the advantage of customer confidence. The more customers trust you, the more sales you can make. 3 Approved documents and the "Original product" mark will also help customers to find your products among hundreds of others: there is a special "Original product" filter in the search engine which will display products with approved documents only. As a result, you get a competitive advantage over other sellers not having the documents. But first, pay attention to the requirements: If you are a trademark holder, you only need to upload the certificate of trademark registration. But if you work with a distributor, your list of documents should show a connection to the trademark holder. The list of documents that you need to provide for Ozon depends on the model of granting selling rights for branded products that you use. You can upload certificates for a brand that is registered in the territory of Russia or has an international registration (WIPO) and is protected in the territory of the Russian Federation. The documents confirming your right to sell the brand must contain: • The document creation date. • The document validity period. Indefinite documents aren’t accepted. • A signature or a stamp of the trademark holder on the document itself. • The right to sell the product in the territory of the Russian Federation. • The documents are accepted in English, Russian, Turkish, and Chinese. Learn about the requirements in our article How to upload a confirming document: 1 Go to the Products and prices → Certificates → Brand certificates88 section and click Add document . 2 Specify the confirming document details and its validity period. Indefinite documents aren’t accepted. 3 Upload a file with an electronic copy of the document with a maximum size of 10 MB. 4 Click Save changes . A brand certificate will be created in the system. After verification, you’ll be able to sell products of the brand on Ozon: the “Original product” mark will appear on PDPs of the brand within a day. Learn how to upload a document in the article
